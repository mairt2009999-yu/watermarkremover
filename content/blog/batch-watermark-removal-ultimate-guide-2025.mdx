## Method 2: Chrome Developer Tools (Highest Quality)

### Step-by-Step Instructions

**Step 1:** Open Instagram in Chrome
- Go to instagram.com
- Find your video/image
- Play the video (important)

**Step 2:** Open Developer Tools
- Press F12 (or right-click → Inspect)
- Click "Network" tab
- Filter by "Media" or type "mp4"

**Step 3:** Download the file
- Refresh page (F5)
- Look for files ending in .mp4 or .jpg
- Right-click → "Open in new tab"
- Right-click video → "Save video as"

**Quality:** Original upload quality
**Works for:** All content types
**Difficulty:** Medium (needs practice)

### Pro Tips for Dev Tools Method
- Sort by "Size" to find video files quickly
- Look for largest MP4 file (usually the video)
- For images, filter by "Img" instead
